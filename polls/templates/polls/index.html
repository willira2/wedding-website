Downtown:

A downtown hotel would be most convenient for people who want to attend the afterparty. Downtown to the venue is a 15 to 20 minute drive. There are many more downtown hotels than just those listed here.

  • The Chattanoogan
  • Chattanooga Choo Choo Hotel
  • Holiday Inn and Suites Chattanooga Downtown

Outside Downtown:

There are several hotels (more than just these listed here) near the base of the mountain that are less expensive than the downtown hotels. From this area to the venue is a 15 minute drive and to downtown takes 10 minutes.

  • Holiday Inn Express and Suites Chattanooga - Lookout Mountain
  • Hampton Inn Chattanooga West - Lookout Mountain
  • La Quinta Inn and Suites

On the Mountain:

There are no hotels on Lookout Mountain, but there are a few B&B businesses. Obviously, these are closest to the venue.

  • Garden Walk Inn
  • Riverview Inn
  • Chanticleer Inn Bed and Breakfast

There are also AirBnBs in the area, including on Lookout Mountain.

Downtown:

  • Tennessee Aquarium - It's like a zoo but for fish.
  • Walnut Street Bridge - Iconic blue pedestrian bridge that links downtown to the Northshore.
  • Hunter Museum of American Art - American art from the Colonial period to today housed in an early 20th century mansion with a 1970's modern addition.
  • Northshore Neighborhood - Eclectic neighborhood with shopping and dining on the other side of the river from downtown.️
  • Chattanooga Riverwalk - Access everything above via the Riverwalk.

On the Mountain:

  • Ruby Falls - Underground waterfall and caves inside Lookout Mountain.
  • Incline Railway - One mile railway track built in 1895 that takes you straight up the side of Lookout Mountain at a 72.7% incline. Jimmy refuses to do it.
  • Point Park - 10 acre memorial park on the end of Lookout that overlooks Lookout Mountain Battlefield and Chattanooga.
  • Reflection Riding Arboretum and Nature Center - Free admission to trails, gardens, and a native animal exhibit

Chattanooga is the birthplace of both the Moon Pie and Krystal's. But in case that's not enough for you, here are some suggestions.

  • Lupi's Pizza - Best pizza in town, don't let anyone try to convince you otherwise.
  • Mojo Burrito - Quick and casual Southwestern done right.
  • Public House Restaurant - Great lunch place that does lighter Southern cuisine (oxymoron?).
  • Terminal Brewhouse - Great for lunch or dinner, with beer brewed on site in a historic Chattanooga landmark.️
  • Il Primo - Excellent italian food in the relaxed Northshore neighborhood.️

How do you say "Fairyland"?

Rachel can tell you that you don't actually say "fairy land" like "fairy tales." It's more like "fair-uh land" with the emphasis on the first syllable. She knows this because she pronounced it the wrong way for a long time before anyone corrected her. Just like when she lived in Horry County for a year before anyone told her the "h" in "Horry" is silent.

UPDATE: Jimmy's Uncle Savage has provided some helpful feedback on this important issue. "Fairyland Club" is one word better pronounced this way: "Fairlunclub"
