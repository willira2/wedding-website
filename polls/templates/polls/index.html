Are there any other events other than ceremony and reception?

Yes, there are two! The reception will be from 1pm to about 4pm. That evening, we have a casual afterparty planned at Brewery downtown. No RSVP necessary. We'll be there starting around 8pm until Rachel either falls asleep or passes out. Feel free to pop in for a drink or two or stay a while and get weird.

Sunday morning, Jimmy's family will be throwing a brunch where y'all are welcome to stop by before heading out of town.

Is there a specific timeline for Saturday?

Ceremony begins at 12pm Central Time. We suggest arriving at least twenty minutes beforehand in order to have time to park and find a seat. Ceremony will take about 20 to 25 minutes, after which y'all are invited to the reception area inside the Fairyland Club for some drinks and light snackage while we finish up a couple of post-ceremony photos (we'll be quick!). Once photos are done, lunch will be served at approximately 1:15-ish.

There will be two bar areas inside, a bluegrass band providing some tunes, and lawn games outside below the porch area. We'll cut the cake around 3-ish and leave at 4. That night we're having an afterparty downtown at Brewery where you can let your hair down until the wee hours of the morning, though Rachel will probably fall asleep by 11.

What's the dresscode?

Well, we'd prefer if you wear clothes. For women, we suggest a sundress/romper/jumpsuit or dressy blouse and pants, and for men, a button down, sports coat, or full suit. Whatever gets that boat floating. Ladies/heel-wearers, unless you go outside to the lawn, there is no grass or gravel you'll walk over, though the parking lot does slope downhill.

The ceremony will be outdoors, and the temperature is usually in the mid-70's in late April.

Really, just wear clothes.

Is there parking at Fairyland Club?

Yes, there's parking right in front of the building, and overflow parking a little down the street. You won't need to worry about finding a spot to park.

How do you say "Fairyland"?

Rachel can tell you that you don't actually say "fairy land" like "fairy tales." It's more like "fair-uh land" with the emphasis on the first syllable. She knows this because she pronounced it the wrong way for a long time before anyone corrected her. Just like when she lived in Horry County for a year before anyone told her the "h" in "Horry" is silent.

What's on the menu for lunch?

Lunch will be buffet style, running from sandwiches to salad to hot sides. We'll try to have a wide enough variety to satisfy most tastes and diet restrictions.

Do you have a registry?

We do not have a registry.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by March 15, 2019, otherwise we will have to hunt you down like an animal.

Will the ceremony be outside or inside?

The ceremony will be outdoors, and the temperature is usually in the mid-70's in late April. Keep in mind that it's usually a few degrees cooler on the mountain than it is downtown.

In case of rain, the ceremony will be moved inside Fairyland Club's ballroom.

Can I bring a guest?

Due to venue capacity we can’t host any additional guests that didn’t receive formal invitations.

Where is the reception?

Same place as the ceremony, Fairyland Club. Weather permitting, we'll do the ceremony beside the tennis courts (#romance) and then move inside for lunch/drinks/bluegrass jammin'.

Open bar??

Yep! There will be an open bar at the reception. Afterparty downtown will not be open bar.
